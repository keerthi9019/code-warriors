Donation Guidelines
What Can Be Donated:
  • Non-perishable items within expiration date
  • Fresh produce that's still good quality
  • Unopened packaged foods
  • Canned goods in good condition
  • Frozen foods (if transportation allows)
What Cannot Be Donated:
  • Expired food items
  • Opened or partially used products
  • Home-cooked meals (unless to approved facilities)
  • Damaged packaging or containers
  • Items stored improperly
Food Safety: Always check with the receiving organization about their specific guidelines and requirements.
